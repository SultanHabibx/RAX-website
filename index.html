WE ARE RAX

RAX Myanmar Co., Ltd is a leading export and import company based in Yangon, Myanmar, specializing in premium fresh seafood and perishable produce for wholesale markets.

We connect Myanmar's finest seafood and agricultural products with businesses across the Middle East and Southeast Asia, ensuring quality, freshness, and reliability in every shipment.

With years of experience in international trade and a deep understanding of cold chain logistics, we've built lasting relationships with suppliers and buyers who trust us to deliver excellence every time.
